B.O.M.B

B.O.M.B (Bombastic Ordinance Mining Bunnies) is a single-player side-scrolling 2D action platformer.

You play as “Brother Bun”, the son of a mining company, BOMB Co. Players must drill through the environment and collect ores to deposit in the collector to progress through the mines.

Avoid hazards, enemies and watch out for your ever-depleting fuel meter!
